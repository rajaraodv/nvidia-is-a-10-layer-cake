What this role exists to figure out

Strategic questions the VP works through with Product, Research, DevRel, Sales, and Coalition partners. Tactics and KPIs follow from the answers.

Q · 01
What's our positioning?
What's the one-line answer to "why Nemotron, not Claude / Qwen / DeepSeek?" Candidate angles to test: "the leading US-based open-source model" · "the best open model for agentic reasoning at frontier throughput" · "the open model that runs everywhere NVIDIA hardware does" · "the multimodal open default." Which one wins?
Q · 02
Who is the ICP — B2B, B2D, or both?
B2B enterprise (Fortune 500 building internal agents), B2B mid-market (Series A–C SaaS companies), or B2D (individual developers, indie builders, OSS maintainers)? Different audiences require different channels, content, pricing, and metrics. Hypothesis: both — but B2D first builds the gravity B2B then converts.
Q · 03
What's the use-case we win?
DeepSeek owns reasoning-at-low-cost. Anthropic owns coding-agents. Qwen owns multilingual. Where can Nemotron be undeniably best? Candidates: agentic reasoning at frontier throughput · multimodal (Nemotron Nano 2 VL) · sovereign / on-prem deployment · coding agents with the Cursor partnership · long-context (1M+ tokens). We pick one to dominate, then expand.
Q · 04
How do we fix launches?
Every Nemotron release should be a category-defining moment. What does the production-quality launch playbook look like — hero video, partner pre-builds, embargoed creator access, tier-1 press, founder/researcher voice on X? Who owns each piece?
Q · 05
How do we collapse the entry surface?
19+ developer portals. Fragmented logins. The first-impression developer often leaves before getting to a working API call. What's the single front door, the single console, and the path from landing to first call in under 5 minutes?
Q · 06
What's the adoption motion?
Free tier hits 40 RPM. Next step today is NVAIE at $1K+/GPU/yr. Nothing in between. Do we add Individual + Team paid tiers? PLG-to-NVAIE qualified-pipeline motion? Where does that fit in the bigger strategy?
Q · 07
Where does NVIDIA show up in dev culture?
Today NVIDIA owns GTC. Anthropic / OpenAI / Mistral show up everywhere else — AI Engineer Summit, KubeCon, Cursor events, Latent Space podcast. What's the conference / hackathon / creator strategy beyond GTC?

What success looks like

To be defined with stakeholders — these are the headlines

12 months — Nemotron in the top 3 open models by OpenRouter token volume, HuggingFace monthly downloads, and AI-engine share-of-voice. One clear positioning claim landed in market.

24 months — #1 open model on at least one of those three. A defined niche where Nemotron is the answer. Self-serve developer pipeline feeding NVAIE.

Specific KPIs (signups, activation rate, retention, conversion to paid, qualified pipeline) flow from the answers to Q01–Q07 above. Locked in the first 60 days alongside the strategy.
