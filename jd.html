What this role exists to figure out — and solve

Three things. Everything else (tactics, KPIs, headcount, budget) flows from these. Worked through with the CMO, Product, Research, DevRel, Sales, and Coalition partners — and driven end-to-end by this VP.

01 · GO-TO-MARKET
Figure out the customer outcome and the go-to-market.
Start with what matters most: why should a developer choose Nemotron? From the customer outcome, everything else falls out.
  • Customer outcome. What can a developer build with Nemotron that they can't anywhere else? The one-line reason they care. This is the work that matters most.
  • ICP. B2D (individual developers, OSS maintainers), B2B mid-market (Series A–C SaaS), B2B enterprise (Fortune 500). Different channels, content, pricing for each. Pick the sequence.
  • Positioning. Against Claude / Qwen / DeepSeek / OpenAI / Mistral. Candidates: "the leading US-based open-source model" · "best at agentic reasoning at frontier throughput" · "the multimodal open default" · "the open model US enterprise trusts." Pick one. Own it.
  • Gaps + competitive landscape. Where we lose today, where we can win. Customers always compare; have the answer ready.
02 · MARKETING AS SUPPLY CHAIN
Make Nemotron stupid-easy to adopt — and impossible to ignore.
Own the work. Orchestrate the teams. Marketing as supply chain — every campaign threads through multiple groups with deadlines, dependencies, one outcome.
  • Engineering, ML, Product. Make it simple to get started. Collapse the 19-portal maze. Get a developer from landing → first API call in under five minutes.
  • Developer Advocacy + DevRel. Compelling videos, hero demos, "how I built X with Nemotron" content. The work that turns "Nemotron is good" into "I tried it and it's incredible."
  • Marketing org (PR, AR, brand, social, growth). Production-quality launches every release. Hero video, embargoed creator network, tier-1 press, founder/researcher voice on X.
  • Hackathons + conferences + creator programs. Show up beyond GTC — AI Engineer Summit, KubeCon, Cursor events, Latent Space podcast. Run our own at a higher bar.
03 · PARTNERS
Turn partners into a global force multiplier.
Make every Nemotron Coalition member, cloud partner, and ISV a marketing channel for Nemotron — without us doing the work twice.
  • Coalition co-marketing. Cursor, LangChain, Mistral, Perplexity, Black Forest Labs, Sarvam, Reflection AI, Thinking Machines. Joint launches, default-routing deals, named partner pre-builds same-day as every release.
  • Customer quotes + adoption stories. Every partner deployment is a credible case study. Mine them, package them, publish them at scale.
  • Co-marketing programs. Make it stupid-easy for partners to take our assets and market on our behalf — globally. Toolkits, briefs, co-funded campaigns, asset libraries.
  • Partner marketing org partnership. Work alongside NVIDIA's existing partner marketing team on the gen-AI surface. Force multiplier, not duplicated effort.

All three serve one thing: the customer outcome. If a developer builds something with Nemotron they couldn't build elsewhere — and tells everyone about it — that's our marketing outcome too.

What success looks like

To be defined with stakeholders — these are the headlines

12 months — Nemotron in the top 3 open models by OpenRouter token volume, HuggingFace monthly downloads, and AI-engine share-of-voice. One clear positioning claim landed in market.

24 months — #1 open model on at least one of those three. A defined niche where Nemotron is the answer. Self-serve developer pipeline feeding NVAIE.

Specific KPIs (signups, activation rate, retention, conversion to paid, qualified pipeline) flow from the answers to Q01–Q07 above. Locked in the first 60 days alongside the strategy.
