What this role does

The marketing goal is one thing: make Nemotron the #1 open-source AI model in the world, measured by developer adoption. To get there, three things have to happen. This VP owns all three — figured out with stakeholders, driven end-to-end.

  • Understand and start fixing the developer funnel. Foundation work: map where Nemotron loses developers today, then ship the fixes against the leakiest stages. Diagnosis and action together.
    • Awareness — most developers don't know Nemotron exists. Diagnose where they look for "the best open model" today, then start landing Nemotron in those channels: AI engines, search, dev podcasts, OSS communities.
    • Trial & activation — from landing to first API call, the path is broken (19+ portals, fragmented logins, the "Public API Endpoints" permission bug). Map it, quantify the dropoffs, work with Product and Engineering to fix the worst leaks first.
    • Product & ICP fit — does the model deliver for the developer who is our ICP? Lock the ICP (B2D individual developers, B2B mid-market SaaS, B2B enterprise — pick the sequence), lock the use cases we genuinely win (coding, reasoning, multimodal, agents, voice, long-context), kill the ones we don't.
    • Positioning & advantage — pick the one-line claim ("leading US-based open-source model" · "best at agentic reasoning" · "multimodal open default" · "open model US enterprise trusts") and start landing it everywhere — site copy, launches, sales decks, partner co-marketing.
  • Run marketing as a supply chain. Make Nemotron stupid-easy to adopt and impossible to ignore.
    • Engineering + ML + Product — collapse the 19-portal maze. Get a developer from landing → first API call in under five minutes.
    • Developer Advocacy + DevRel — compelling videos, hero demos, "how I built X with Nemotron" content. Turn "Nemotron is good" into "I tried it and it's incredible."
    • Flagship open-source reference agents — sales, support, RevOps, real-world agents built on NemoClaw + Nemotron. Fully working, forkable, the working demos competitors haven't shipped. Make Nemotron the model behind every flagship open agent.
    • Marketing org (PR, AR, brand, social, growth) — production-quality launches every release. Hero video, creator network, tier-1 press, founder/researcher voice on X.
    • Hackathons + conferences + creators — show up beyond GTC. AI Engineer Summit, KubeCon, Cursor events, Latent Space podcast. Run our own at a higher bar.
  • Turn partners into a global force multiplier. Coalition, clouds, and ISVs as marketing channels — not duplicated work.
    • Coalition co-marketing — Cursor, LangChain, Mistral, Perplexity, Black Forest Labs, Sarvam, Reflection, Thinking Machines. Joint launches and named partner pre-builds same-day as every release.
    • Customer quotes + adoption stories — mine every partner deployment. Package, publish, repeat.
    • Co-marketing programs — make it stupid-easy for partners to take our assets and market on our behalf, globally.
    • Partner marketing org partnership — work alongside NVIDIA's existing partner marketing team. Force multiplier, not redundant effort.
All three serve one thing — the customer outcome. If a developer builds something with Nemotron they couldn't build elsewhere and tells everyone about it, that's our marketing outcome too.

What success looks like

  • 12 months. Nemotron in the top 3 open-source models by OpenRouter token volume, HuggingFace monthly downloads, and AI-engine share-of-voice. One clear positioning claim landed in market.
  • 24 months. #1 open-source model on at least one of those three. A defined niche where Nemotron is the undeniable answer. Self-serve developer pipeline feeding NVAIE.

Specific KPIs (signups, activation, retention, conversion, qualified pipeline) locked in the first 60 days alongside the strategy.
