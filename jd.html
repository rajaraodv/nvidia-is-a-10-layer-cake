About the role

NVIDIA is the world's leading AI infrastructure company, and Nemotron is our open-frontier model family — the wedge that drives developer adoption of the broader NVIDIA stack. We are hiring a Vice President of Developer Marketing to own the end-to-end developer marketing strategy for Nemotron and our generative AI platform, from awareness through adoption.

This is a strategic role with hands-on execution. You will design the winning developer marketing system, orchestrate it across Product, Research, DevRel, Partners, and Sales, and ship it personally where speed matters. Success is measured in developer mindshare moved — leaderboard position, real adoption, and the chip demand that follows.

The challenge

What this role exists to solve.

  • Nemotron is technically top-tier but commercially invisible. Top of Artificial Analysis's efficiency-adjusted intelligence index, yet outside the top 20 on OpenRouter by developer-billed token volume.
  • Developer mindshare is owned by competitors. Anthropic owns the agent protocol stack and IDE defaults. Qwen owns Hugging Face. DeepSeek owns reasoning-at-low-cost. We don't own a category.
  • Activation is broken. 19+ developer-facing portals, multiple logins, fragmented onboarding. The first-impression developer leaves before getting to a working API call.
  • Launches under-deliver. Spec-sheet rollouts where a category-defining moment is required. Same-day partners are mid-market dev tools where competitors get Notion / Stripe / Adobe.
  • NVIDIA's chip business depends on this. Every Nemotron adoption is a leading indicator of GPU demand. Losing the open-model layer means losing the wedge.

Strategies you'll own

The plays this role designs and drives.

  1. Positioning & counter-positioning. Land the one-line answer to "why Nemotron, not Claude / Qwen / DeepSeek?" — anchored in outcomes developers care about, not architecture.
  2. Awareness engine. Production-quality launches, creator and influencer reach, AI-engine share-of-voice, tier-1 press relationships.
  3. Activation system. Consolidate the entry surface. One developer console, one onboarding, time-to-first-call under five minutes.
  4. Adoption flywheel. Self-serve → individual paid → team → NVAIE enterprise. PLG-to-enterprise motion that turns dev adoption into qualified pipeline.
  5. Distribution moats. Default model in IDE / agent tools (Cursor, Windsurf, Zed). MCP-native posture. Coalition partner co-marketing.
  6. Community & ecosystem. Indie builder grants, hackathons, conference presence beyond GTC, an open contributor program.

Tactics you'll execute

The concrete output of the role — quarter to quarter.

  • Apple-keynote-grade launch videos for every Nemotron release. Hero demos that define the narrative, not benchmark slides.
  • Pre-briefed creator and influencer network. Embargoed access for Latent Space, AI Explained, Berman, Two Minute Papers, AssemblyAI. Tier-1 press pre-briefs (Bloomberg, NYT, TechCrunch, Forbes).
  • Named partner pre-builds shipped same-day as every flagship launch — through the Nemotron Coalition (Cursor, LangChain, Mistral, Perplexity, etc.).
  • Founder / researcher voice on X. Five-to-eight named NVIDIA AI leads with active developer-Twitter presence, posting benchmark drops, training tricks, "weights live" threads.
  • Standalone framework-agnostic Nemotron Cookbook — runnable Jupyter notebooks, no NVIDIA-stack assumption, equivalent to Anthropic and OpenAI's cookbooks.
  • Default-routing deals with Cursor, Windsurf, Zed, and Claude Code for Nemotron as the default open model.
  • 20+ first-party MCP servers across the Nemotron / NIM / Triton / Run:ai / AI Workbench / Brev stack.
  • "Built with Nemotron" grant and bounty program — indie dev grants, OSS maintainer bounties, conference and creator sponsorships.
  • Quarterly hackathons + non-NVIDIA conference presence at AI Engineer Summit, KubeCon, PyData, Latent Space, ICLR.
  • Programmatic SEO + Answer Engine Optimization at scale across high-intent developer queries.
  • Developer pricing tiers — partner with Product / Pricing to introduce Individual and Team plans between free and NVAIE.
  • Always-on growth experiments across the funnel — onboarding, pricing, conversion, retention.
