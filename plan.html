§ 3 · The central insight

We don't have a model problem. We have a launch & positioning problem.

The pattern
Nemotron 3 Super is technically #1 on Artificial Analysis's efficiency-adjusted intelligence index — yet didn't break out beyond NVIDIA's existing dev/enterprise audience. The launch surfaces simply don't match the model's substance. OpenAI launches a story with the model embedded. NVIDIA launched a spec sheet with distribution attached.

§ 4 · What this VP owns

Four pillars. End-to-end ownership.

Strategy at the top. Production-quality execution underneath. Supply-chain orchestration in the middle. Hands-on building when it's faster than waiting.

A.
Strategy — set the winning play
Diagnose · Position · Plan
  • JOURNEYOwn the entire developer journey end-to-end. From "I've never heard of Nemotron" to "I'm shipping production on it" — map every step, instrument every dropoff, ship fixes against the leakiest stages.
  • POSITIONPositioning & counter-positioning. Why Nemotron, not Claude / DeepSeek / Qwen / Mistral? Land the one-line answer in market. Open is table stakes; outcome is the moat.
  • HACKSGrowth hacks for awareness AND adoption. AEO, programmatic SEO at scale, viral demos, ABM. Awareness ≠ adoption — both lanes need different tactics, both need shipping.
  • PLANThe 6-12 month strategic plan. Quarterly OKRs. Cross-functional alignment. Communicated to Alison, exec staff, and the broader marketing org.
B.
Production-quality launches — turn every release into a moment
Hooks · Videos · Demos · Reach
  • HOOKPartner with Product to define the hook for every release. Not benchmark slides — the one-line claim plus the one-screenshot proof that journalists, creators, and developers will repeat.
  • VIDEOHighest production quality launch videos. Apple-keynote-grade. The launch IS the film; the press cycle, partner integrations, and creator reactions hang off it.
  • DEMOHero demos and compelling apps that stand out from the crowd. Built in-house when needed. Tied to outcomes a developer can't get with any other model. The "wow" moment that becomes the meme.
  • INFLUCreator & influencer program. Pre-briefed embargoes for Latent Space, AI Explained, Berman, Two Minute Papers, AssemblyAI, Mr. Beast for consumer reach. Land the reaction at hour zero.
  • PRTier-1 press pre-briefs. Bloomberg, NYT, AP, TechCrunch, Forbes, WIRED. Worked the way major product companies work them.
  • PARTNERSSame-day named partner pre-builds. The OpenAI playbook — every flagship launch ships with Notion/Stripe/Adobe/Shopify-tier names already integrated. NVIDIA can do this through the Coalition; today we don't.
C.
The supply chain — orchestrate every team
Cross-functional · Coordinated · Always-on
  • PRODProduct partnership. NIM, Nemotron, build.nvidia.com, NGC PMs. Co-design hooks. Influence model release timing for narrative impact.
  • RESEARCHNVIDIA Research + ML team. Pull Bryan Catanzaro, Mostofa Patwary, and other Nemotron leads onto the launch front line. Their voices on X matter more than corporate handles ever will.
  • DEVRELDeveloper Advocates & community team. Coordinate field activation. Their relationships become the conduits for every campaign, hackathon, and Coalition co-event.
  • EVENTSConferences and hackathons. Run NVIDIA's own (GTC slot for gen-AI dev, "Nemotron Day") and show up loudly at AI Engineer Summit, KubeCon, PyData, Latent Space, ICLR/NeurIPS. Hackathons quarterly — with prize structures that produce shareable content.
  • COALITIONCoalition partner co-marketing. Cursor, LangChain, Mistral, Perplexity, Sarvam, BFL, Reflection, TML. Convert membership into shipped, co-branded developer wins. Quarterly partner-launch cadence.
  • SALESPLG → Enterprise (NVAIE) handoff. Connect self-serve dev adoption to enterprise inside-sales pipeline. The Redis playbook, applied at NVIDIA scale.
D.
Hands-on — build what's missing
Founder energy at VP scope
  • AGENTSAI-first operating model. Internal agents for funnel analytics, ABM, content, AEO research, partner ops, Gong analysis. Force-multiply a small team using NVIDIA's own gen-AI stack.
  • CONTENTShip content myself when speed matters. Cookbook recipes, sample apps, hero videos, growth pages. Don't wait for an agency. The 10-Layer Cake and the AI animated keynote — built in 48 hours each. That's the operating tempo.
  • DEMOSBuild the "wow" demo personally when the team's at capacity. A compelling Nemotron demo built and shipped fast beats a perfect demo built slow. Hands-on prototype, then hand off for polish.
  • EXPERIMENTSAlways-on growth experiments. A/B test the funnel. Pricing tests. Onboarding flows. AEO scrapes. Programmatic SEO sweeps. Output, not vanity — same approach that lifted Kumo SoV 2% → 9.5% in 30 days.
