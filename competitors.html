§ 2 · Anthropic
Anthropic Own the agent protocol stack "The model developers trust + the protocol everyone builds on."
Protocol moat
MCP (Model Context Protocol) — Anthropic invented it. OpenAI extended it via ChatGPT Apps SDK. Cursor, Windsurf, Zed, VS Code, Claude Code are all MCP-compatible. The standard for tool-calling and agent integrations in 2026.
Cookbook standard
claude-cookbooks on GitHub — Jupyter notebooks, every recipe runnable, mirrored at platform.claude.com/cookbook. Cited by developers as best-in-class.
$100M Partner Network
Launched March 12, 2026. Direct training, sales enablement, co-marketing dollars to partners. Accenture (30K trained), Deloitte (470K seats), PwC, KPMG, Cognizant, Infosys, Slalom, Tribe AI, Turing.
IDE / agent-tool defaults
Claude is the default model in Cursor, powers Windsurf's Cascade, integrates with Zed. Most builders see Claude before they see any other model.
Connector ecosystem
9 creative-tool connectors (April 2026): Blender, Adobe, Autodesk, Ableton, Splice. Thomson Reuters CoCounsel MCP integration (May 2026). The "Claude works inside your tool" surface is enormous.
Founder voice on X
Dario Amodei, Jared Kaplan, Mike Krieger. Technical, terse, benchmark-forward. Anthropic doesn't sound like a corporation; it sounds like the people who built the thing.
Signature for us to steal MCP-native posture + a standalone Cookbook + IDE-default deals are the holy trinity. Without those three, no amount of cloud-partner distribution closes the developer mindshare gap.
§ 3 · DeepSeek
DeepSeek The model is the marketing "Zero traditional spend. Maximum virality."
Stealth launches
V4 Pro + V4 Flash launched April 24, 2026 under MIT. No press tour. No analyst briefings. Weights drop on HF/GitHub on a Friday afternoon. Twitter does the rest.
Price as marketing
V4 Pro: $0.435/M input · $0.87/M output with 80.6% SWE-bench Verified (within 0.2 of Claude Opus 4.6 at $25/M output). 7× price gap at parity coding performance. That's the launch campaign.
OpenRouter ubiquity
23 DeepSeek models on OpenRouter. V4 Flash is free across 13 providers. The "default fallback" model when devs hit any rate limit.
Researcher-voice tweets
Named DeepSeek researchers post benchmark screenshots within hours of release. "Sputnik moment" effect — global press follows the developer Twitter conversation.
HF reproduction velocity
Open-R1 hit 10K GitHub stars in 3 days after the open-source reproduction effort began. HF community treats every DeepSeek release as an event.
MIT license framing
Not a legal argument — a signal. "We're not going to play games with you" — picked up by every dev-Twitter explainer thread.
Signature for us to steal You don't market open models — you ship distribution. Pricing, presence on 10+ providers, and benchmark-screenshot virality cost almost nothing compared to traditional marketing and move 10× the needle.
§ 4 · Qwen / Alibaba
Qwen Hugging Face is the homepage "Be the global open-source default, especially outside English."
Distribution dominance
~1 billion HF downloads by April 2026 — over 50% of all open-source model downloads worldwide. Overtook Llama in Oct 2025.
Language coverage as moat
Qwen 3.6 supports 201 languages and dialects — Javanese, Cebuano, Waray. Owns markets US labs ignore. Indonesian, Indian, SEA, MENA developer communities default to Qwen.
Apache 2.0 as positioning
Not framed as legal language — as zero-friction signal. "Build whatever you want." Comparable to DeepSeek's MIT.
Enterprise adoption claim
90,000+ enterprise adopters cited. Airbnb CEO publicly credits Qwen reliance. That kind of testimonial is the marketing.
Aliyun bundling
Bundled into Alibaba Cloud international regions for enterprise pull. Cloud distribution is paired with open weights — both rails active.
English-side ramp
Twitter/X English presence ramped sharply through 2025-26. Model-launch threads + HF-link drops in the same pattern as DeepSeek.
Signature for us to steal HuggingFace is the homepage, not docs.nvidia.com. The path from "I heard about a new model" to "I'm running it" must be one click on HF — not a journey across 19 NVIDIA portals.
§ 5 · OpenAI
OpenAI Compound consumer brand into developer mindshare "The flywheel only OpenAI has."
Developer count
4M developers · API usage 6B+ tokens/minute (DevDay 2025 disclosures, doubled YoY). The world's largest paid-developer base.
The Cookbook standard
openai-cookbook is the format every other lab copies. Notebook-first, scenario-organized, runnable.
ChatGPT Apps SDK
Built on top of MCP. Launch partners include Booking.com, Coursera, Spotify, Adobe Photoshop, GitHub, Replit, Gmail, Drive, Teams, Mailchimp, Stripe. Notion and Linear as connectors. Slack as first enterprise platform.
Co-developed protocols
Stripe Agentic Commerce Protocol — co-developed and shipped with OpenAI. Salesforce Oct 2025 strategic expansion. OpenAI shows up co-authoring the standards developers use.
DevDay rhythm
Annual marquee. DevDay 2026 set for Sept 29, SF. Every announcement compounds into the next 6 months of dev coverage.
API ↔ ChatGPT flywheel
Unmatched. Every ChatGPT consumer use case becomes a developer template. Every developer integration deepens consumer engagement. Vertical compounding NVIDIA can't directly replicate — but can borrow the rhythm.
Signature for us to steal A DevDay-class annual rhythm, an industry-leading Cookbook, and co-developed protocols with anchor partners (the Stripe Agentic Commerce Protocol equivalent — but for NVIDIA + Coalition).

§ 7 · What works for open models in 2026 (synthesized)

Seven tactics that explain why some open models win and others don't.

Synthesized from the five playbooks above. Each is concrete, observable, and copyable.

01
HuggingFace + GitHub as primary distribution. Qwen and DeepSeek treat HF as the homepage, not the company site. Every developer-acquisition path starts on HF.
02
OpenRouter ubiquity. Be free-tier on multiple providers. DeepSeek V4 Flash on 13 providers means it's the "default fallback" whenever any other model hits a rate limit.
03
Be the default model in Cursor / Windsurf / Claude Code. Anthropic owns this surface. Owning even one IDE default is worth tens of millions of impressions per month.
04
Founder / researcher voice on X. Named individuals dropping benchmark screenshots and "weights are live" tweets — DeepSeek, Anthropic, Mistral. Corporate handles don't generate this energy.
05
Cookbook as primary doc product. Runnable Jupyter notebooks (Anthropic, OpenAI) — not API ref pages. Framework-agnostic and copy-pasteable.
06
Pricing / free-tier as the loudest channel. DeepSeek's 7× price gap was the campaign. No paid amplification needed.
07
MCP-native posture day one. Anthropic invented it. OpenAI extended it. Any 2026 model that isn't MCP-first feels legacy.
§ 8 · So where does this leave us?

NVIDIA is competing on the wrong axis.

Nemotron's distribution to clouds is excellent. Its distribution to developer surfaces — IDE defaults, MCP-native tooling, framework-agnostic cookbook, OpenRouter routing share, indie builder grants — is barely started.

The five competitors above all moved on the dev surfaces first, the cloud distribution second. NVIDIA has done the opposite. The result is the leaderboard you see in competitive.html: technically competitive, commercially invisible.
